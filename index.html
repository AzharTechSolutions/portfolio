I'm Azhar Hanif, Founder & CEO of Azhar Hanif Studio based in Bahawalpur, Pakistan. I graduated with a BS in Information Technology and have since shipped 10+ production apps on the Google Play Store.

My work spans the full product lifecycle — Flutter UI, Firebase/Supabase backends, REST APIs, Riverpod & BLoC state management, clean architecture, Kotlin DSL build pipelines, and Play Store submission. I build for clients on Fiverr & Upwork, and build my own products under Kravix Studio.

I specialise in complex multi-role systems, real-time data flows, payment gateway integrations (Hesabe), full RTL/EN localisation, and premium dark UI patterns that actually feel luxurious.
